RaceDriver is a retro-inspired arcade racer with chunky pixels,
custom-painted road and cars, hazards, pickups, and a C64-style palette.

Features
- Classic countdown → race → game over flow
- Hazards (oil/puddles), pickups (fuel/life/nitro/coin)
- Nitro boost, slipstream, near-miss combos
- Day/night cycle, road width dynamics, level progression

Tip: Swipe to steer.
